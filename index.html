C&E Policy?

At BlipdIn LLC, we have a saying. Making apps/websites for clients
is awesome but making apps/websites just
for the heck of making apps/websites is down right mindblowing.
So, the developers during their free time spend time on their personal pet projects.
There's just one rule they follow: The end product must be helpful in someway to the people.
And it is from this ideology that the Create & Educate policy was born.
Create apps & websites that educate people.
Three such projects have been born out of this policy
so far educating people in the fields of technology and academics.
